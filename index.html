Neuroscience and Neuroengineering Laboratory (NNRL)

About NNRL

The Neuroscience and Neuroengineering Laboratory (NNRL) at Iran University of Science and Technology (IUST) in Tehran is dedicated to advancing research in neuroscience. Our work focuses on areas such as working memory and decoding neural signals like LFP, EEG, and fMRI. Equipped with state-of-the-art tools like eye trackers and EEG recording systems, NNRL aims to unravel the complexities of the brain through innovative experiments and analysis.

About NNRL

Neuroscience and Neuroengineering Research Laboratory

Neuroscience and Neuroengineering Research Laboratory (NNRL) was founded by Electrical Engineering faculty members of IUST (Iran University of Science and Technology) in 2014.

Research Areas:
  • Fundamental Neuroscience and Cognitive Science research
  • Designing BCI systems for rehabilitation of people with nervous system disease or injury
  • Early-stage detection of neural system diseases including Seizure, Parkinson's disease, MS, Alzheimer, ALS, etc.
